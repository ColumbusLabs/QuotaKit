# Privacy Policy

**QuotaKit iOS** — Last updated: 2026-06-07

## Summary

QuotaKit iOS does not collect, sell, or share personal data with Columbus Labs or third-party analytics services.

## Data Collection

We do not collect analytics or operate a backend for QuotaKit iOS. Specifically:

- **No analytics or tracking** — no third-party SDKs, no telemetry, no crash reporting services.
- **No personal information** — we do not ask for or store your name, email, or any identifiers.
- **No Columbus Labs backend** — the app does not send quota data to Columbus Labs servers.

## iCloud Sync

The app uses Apple's iCloud CloudKit to receive usage data from the companion macOS app on the same Apple ID. Each Mac maintains its own device record, and the iPhone merges data from all connected Macs. This data, including AI provider usage metrics, stays within your private iCloud account and is not sent to Columbus Labs.

## On-Device Only

QuotaKit iOS processes synced usage data locally on your device. Columbus Labs does not run a server that receives or stores your QuotaKit usage data.
