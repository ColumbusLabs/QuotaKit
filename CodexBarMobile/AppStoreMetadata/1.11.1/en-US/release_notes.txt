QuotaKit 1.11.1 adds richer provider details, clearer quota data, and more reliable sync from QuotaKit for Mac.

What's new:
- CrossModel balances and daily, weekly, and monthly usage, spend, token, and request details now sync to iPhone.
- Codex credit-limit windows now appear in companion app and widget data.
- Provider ordering is clearer, with Sakana and Wayfinder branded consistently.
- Quota reset times and large token totals are more accurate, with stronger multi-account sync and widget background refresh handling.

QuotaKit for iPhone requires QuotaKit for Mac with iCloud Sync enabled.
