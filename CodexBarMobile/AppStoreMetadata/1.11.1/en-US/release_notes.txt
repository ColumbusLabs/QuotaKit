QuotaKit 1.11.1 adds provider logo polish across the app and widgets, improves widget sync from background CloudKit updates, and keeps the QuotaKit Pro widget experience aligned with the Mac app.

What's new:
- Provider rows, usage cards, detail views, dashboard provider-share lists, and widgets now use the same provider logo assets as QuotaKit for Mac.
- Widgets can refresh their stored snapshot directly from background CloudKit silent pushes, so new Mac sync data can update the widget without opening the app first.
- Recent 1.11.1 updates also added in-app widget provider selection, provider reordering, and widget quota-window controls for Both, Session, or Weekly views.

QuotaKit for iPhone requires QuotaKit for Mac with iCloud Sync enabled.
